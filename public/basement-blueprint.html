Layout Summary (rev 3)

Room extended ~25% N-S to ~6.9m to fit all zones with proper spacing. A N-S dividing wall (~5in thick) splits the basement into west (HVAC room) and east (bedroom). The wall is L-shaped: it runs N-S from the north wall, then turns ~3ft east to meet a door frame, creating the transition into a ~3.5ft hallway that separates the bedroom from the south section.

East/Bedroom: Bed (on floor) with west edge against dividing wall, clothes box (U-Haul) south of bed also against the wall. Small gap/opening between clothes box and the L-wall turn.

West/HVAC Room: HVAC furnace close to the north raised platform (centered). Utility sink on the west wall ~5ft south of HVAC, oriented N-S (one basin north, one south). Window above sink.

South section: Stairs descend from SE corner. Open landing west of stairs. Paint closet west of landing, extending west to align with dividing wall. Open shelving south of paint closet with no west wall — opens directly into the HVAC/sink area.
